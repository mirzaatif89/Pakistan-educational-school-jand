Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading educational institution that inspires young minds with knowledge, character, innovation, and Islamic values, empowering every learner to become a confident, compassionate, and responsible leader who contributes positively to Pakistan and the world.

Mission

We are committed to providing quality, affordable, and student-centered education in a safe and nurturing environment. We develop academic excellence, moral values, critical thinking, creativity, leadership, and lifelong learning through modern teaching practices, strong parent partnerships, and dedicated educators.

CEO's Message

Pakistan Educational School System (PESS) Jand

Dear Students, Parents, Teachers, and Community Members,

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to Pakistan Educational School System (PESS) Jand, an institution committed to providing quality education, strong moral values, and a nurturing environment where every child can grow with confidence.

At PESS Jand, we believe that education is not only about academic excellence but also about building character, leadership, creativity, and a lifelong passion for learning. Our dedicated faculty strives to prepare students to face future challenges with knowledge, integrity, and responsibility.

We value the trust that parents place in us and consider them our partners in shaping the future of our children. Together, we will continue to inspire young minds and empower them to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to Pakistan and the world.

As we move forward, our commitment remains unwavering to provide a safe, modern, and inspiring learning environment where every student has the opportunity to achieve excellence.

Thank you for being part of the PESS Jand family. We look forward to a future filled with success, innovation, and outstanding achievements.

Building Knowledge, Shaping Character & Creating a Bright Pakistan.
Engr. Malik Azmat Abbas Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Pakistan Educational School System (PESS) Jand
