SMTP Setup

Add these values in `.env` and restart the server:
SMTP_HOST=
SMTP_PORT=587
SMTP_SECURE=false
SMTP_USER=
SMTP_PASS=
SMTP_FROM_EMAIL=
SMTP_FROM_NAME=Apexiueum

Notes

Recipient emails can be separated with commas.
Students and teachers only appear in quick select if their email is saved in the system.
